    Oren Klopfer: Technologist, Academic, and World Traveler

    Welcome to the online home of Oren Klopfer, a multi-talented individual with a passion for coding, design, and exploring the world. As a dedicated student majoring in Political Sociology and Judaic studies at Hampshire College, Oren is deeply committed to understanding the intersection of society, culture, and politics. When he's not hitting the books, Oren works at the prestigious Yiddish Book Center, helping to preserve and share the rich heritage of Yiddish literature. Additionally, he channels his creative energy into graphic design projects, resulting in eye-catching visuals that communicate ideas and concepts with style and clarity. But there's so much more to Oren than just his academic and professional pursuits. Here are some interesting facts that paint a fuller picture of his multifaceted personality and interests:


    • Animal lover: Oren is the proud owner of 3 cats, 2 dogs, and a hamster, forming a delightful menagerie that keeps him entertained and busy in equal measure.
    • Linux aficionado: Oren maintains two Linux distributions, Rhino Linux and Ubuntu Touch for Pine, proving his expertise in the world of open-source operating systems.
    • Web developer extraordinaire: Among the numerous websites Oren has built, he was specially recruited to construct education.mit.edu, an impressive feat that showcases his technical prowess.
    • Avid traveler: With four continents under his belt, Oren has visited countries such as Canada, England, Sweden, Denmark, Netherlands, Iceland, New Zealand, and Japan, enriching his life through diverse experiences and cultural exchanges.
    • Proud Massachusetts resident: Oren has called Massachusetts home for his entire life, drawing inspiration from its vibrant communities and storied history.
    • Gaming conference speaker: Since the age of 9, Oren has been giving talks at gaming conferences, demonstrating his passion for gaming and his ability to engage audiences on this popular subject.

    Get to know Oren better by exploring his website, and don't hesitate to reach out with any questions or inquiries. As a talented technologist, academic, and adventurer, he's always eager to forge new connections and tackle exciting projects.
